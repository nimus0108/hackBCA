hackBCA IV:
Lighting the Torch

hackBCA IV is the fourth annual hackathon hosted at the Bergen County Academies. hackBCA is open to all high school students who are interested in exploring computer science. Prior programming experience is not needed: hackBCA is an open floor to anyone who wants to create.
